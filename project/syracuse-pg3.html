We Rise Above The Streets

Who?

We Rise Above the Streets is a non profit organization working to aid the homeless in Syracuse.

What?

We Rise Above the Streets works with the community by donating food, water, hygeine products, and other life essentials to those in need.

Habitat For Humanity

Who?

Habitat For Humanity is a non-profit organization working to create houses for the homeless population in America.

What?

Volunteers at H4H work together to create homes from scratch. They also do home improvement projects for individuals who cannot afford it.

The Samaritan Center

Who?

The Samaritan Center is a non-profit organization based in Syracuse that works to decrease the poverty rate by serving free meals everyday.

What?

They utilize team of volunteers to serve approx 120,00 meals annually. They foster a judgement free community for all individuals.
